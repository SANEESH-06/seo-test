OG Image Instructions
=====================

Create an image named "og-image.png" with these specifications:

Dimensions: 1200 x 630 pixels
Format: PNG or JPG
File size: Under 1MB

Content suggestions:
- Your blog logo/name "DevBlog"
- Tagline: "Web Development Tutorials"
- Clean, professional design
- High contrast colors
- Readable text

Tools to create:
- Canva (free templates)
- Figma
- Photoshop
- Online OG image generators

Place the file in: public/og-image.png
